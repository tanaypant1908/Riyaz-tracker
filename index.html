What is Indian Classical Music?

One of the oldest unbroken musical traditions in the world, characterized by its focus on melody (Raga) and rhythm (Tala).

What is a Raga?

A melodic framework for improvisation and composition. Each raga has a specific set of rules, notes, and an emotional soul.

The Importance of Riyaz

Riyaz is the dedicated practice required to master the breath, voice, or instrument. Consistency is the key to musical depth.
